[03]The Challenge

Oklahoma's Children Need Protection

1 in 3 girls and 1 in 5 boys in Oklahoma will be victims of sexual abuse before their 18th birthday. Additionally, one person dies by suicide every 11 hours in our state.

Our Solution

Sara's Project provides a coordinated, multidisciplinary approach bringing together law enforcement, social services, medical professionals, and advocacy staff to ensure comprehensive crisis intervention and support for children and families affected by trauma.

[04]

What We

We deliver comprehensive programs designed to protect children and support families experiencing crisis in Southern Oklahoma.
